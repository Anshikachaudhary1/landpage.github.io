FAQ

  • What is a VPN?

    A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a technology that encrypts your internet connection and makes it more secure and private.

  • Why do I need a VPN?

    A VPN can protect your online privacy, hide your IP address, and allow you to access content that may be restricted in your region.

  • How does Express VPN work?

    Express VPN encrypts your internet connection and routes it through a secure server, which makes it more difficult for third parties to intercept your data or track your online activity.
